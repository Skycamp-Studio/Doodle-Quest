Rift Hero

By SkyCamp Studio

About the Game

Rift Hero is a rogue-like FPS home defense game designed for mixed reality, featuring home invasion from enemies through different apocalypses. Defenders can upgrade and build their own combination of mech weapons to survive the horde.

Features

  • True Survival Horde Made for Mixed Reality: Frenetic home invasion through spatial rifts into your living room, gear up with teleported mech arms, and build in-home fortifications to defend!
  • Hordes of Enemies from Different Apocalypses: A vast variety of zombies and monsters packed for roguelike massacre, featuring a series of realistic battle scenes with unique challenges!
  • Addictive Vampire Survivor-Style Gameplay: Grab EXP gems quickly to get lottery-like power-ups. Design, unlock, and upgrade your mech system with 12 distinct weapons and machineries!
