THE LOCK

Mixed Reality Puzzle Adventure

About the Game

THE LOCK is a mixed reality puzzle adventure featuring brain-teasing lock puzzles that can be deciphered through magical locomotion and spatial teleportation. In the mysterious world of THE LOCK you will find yourself immersed with:

  • 30+ mind-teasing, carefully crafted puzzles, intricately devised through 4 layers of spatial complexity. Each puzzle has a unique theme accompanied by a soothing music soundtrack and an ancient motto to unfold their own mystery.
  • Unique spatial teleportation mechanism that takes the player through multiple layers of geometry to solve complex puzzles, where the player needs to plunge inside and zoom outside between the virtual and real world (like in the movie “Inception”).
  • A puzzler that is super easy to learn, both comfortable and relaxing to play. Playable either in the comfort of your home (MR mode) or in the middle of a mysterious ocean (VR mode). Intuitive hand-tracking is also supported.
